Get to know me

I grew up in Western Springs Illinois, which is about fifteen minutes outside of Chicago. My family and friends have always been the most important part of my life and have exposed me to many hobbies and interests. My grandmothers on both sides of my family were artists, so growing up I spent a lot of time tapping into my creativity with drawing and painting. My parents helped me develop love for travel as a kid, and since then I have visited many amazing countries both with my family and student travel groups.

Friends and Family

The people I surround myself with have been so integral in my personal development. I attribute most of my values to my amazing parents, who have been together for 40 years. My sister Lea is older than me by 4 years and is my closest confidant. I've maintained really close relationships with some of my childhood friends, and have met some amazing friends in college. I currently live with my 5 best friends who I met through my sorority.

Me in Chicago

Though I grew up outside of Chicago, I had the opportunity to live downtown this past summer, which really deepened my love for the city.

Travel

Seeing the world and understanding different cultures is super important to me. I have studied the Spanish language my whole life, so I've made an effort to travel to countries where the primary spoken language is Spanish!

Momi, Western Fiji

Fiji is the most beautiful country I've been to. Most people think of it as just a beachy island, but it is actually a very mountainous country!

Parismina, Costa Rica

I actually camped during my stay in Costa Rica. It is definitely a country where you can really immerse yourself in nature. Panthers, monkeys and sloths were some of the notable wildlife I encountered there.

Florence, Italy

Italy is the only country in Europe I've had the pleasure of visiting. I went on a school trip, and we stayed in Rome, Florence and Venice. Florence was my favorite of the three cities, as it is home to some amazing artwork.
